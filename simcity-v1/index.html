❓ How to play

  • Goal: grow a thriving city. Zone land, provide power, water and services, and keep the budget positive.
  • Camera (3D): right-drag to pan · Shift+right-drag to rotate/tilt · Q/E rotate · wheel zoom · click the minimap. Toggle the classic 2D view with the 🧊/🗺️ button.
  • Camera (2D): right/middle-drag to pan · mouse wheel to zoom · arrow keys.
  • Build: pick a tool on the left, click (or drag) on the map. Bulldoze mistakes with 🚜 (B).
  • Zones (R/C/I) need power (connect with ⚡ lines to a plant) and a road within 4 tiles to develop. Water coverage (💧 pumps/towers) unlocks high density.
  • Residents need jobs (C/I), low pollution and low crime. Services (police, fire, health, schools) raise land value and safety — but cost money monthly.
  • Budget: set taxes ~7%. Too high kills demand; too low bankrupts you. Bonds give quick cash at 6% interest.
  • Disasters: fires spread — build fire stations. Or unleash a monster on a friend's city 😈.
  • Multiplayer: 🌐 → Create room → share the link. Everyone builds on the same city with a shared treasury.
  • Hotkeys: I inspect · B bulldoze · R road · P power line · T trees · 1/2/3 zones · Space pause · +/- zoom · Q/E rotate (3D).
